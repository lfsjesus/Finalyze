Manage Your Finances

Track all your incomes and expenses, and monitor your savings goals — all in one place. The history view allows sorting by date and amount, while a progress bar at the top shows how close you are to achieving your targets.

  • ✓ View and edit past transactions
  • ✓ Sort by date or value
  • ✓ Create short or long-term savings goals
  • ✓ Visual progress tracking with a savings bar
  • ✓ Smart reminders to stay consistent

Understand Your Spending

Break down your expenses into categories and visualize how your money is allocated. Use the insights to adjust your habits and improve your budget management.

  • ✓ Categorization
  • ✓ Fixed vs. variable analysis
  • ✓ Periodic comparisons (1–12 months)

Earn Awards

Transform your financial journey into a game. Get rewarded with points and badges for building healthy habits, hitting savings milestones, and staying consistent.

  • ✓ Points for logging entries
  • ✓ Progress-based achievements
  • ✓ Boost motivation with gamification
